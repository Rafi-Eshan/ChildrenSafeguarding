Children’s Safeguarding in Practice: Your Questions Answered

A Live Q&A Webinar

Chanel Johnson

Founder of Higher Level Consultancy

A practical and interactive safeguarding Q&A webinar where we’ll talk openly, share experiences, and make sense of safeguarding together, led by someone who’s been there, done the work, and still deeply cares about getting it right.

It’s a space to ask your questions, share your thoughts, and learn in a supportive, judgement-free space.

About the Host

I’m the founder of Higher Level Consultancy, a training and consultancy company that focuses on child safeguarding. I’m also a qualified social worker and manager with over 10 years of frontline experience in local authority children’s services. I’m passionate about helping professionals feel more confident in recognising and responding to safeguarding concerns. I know how complex and challenging this work can be, so I focus on making my training real, reflective and rooted in everyday practice. Through HLC, my goal is to make safeguarding feel more accessible, because it really is everyone’s responsibility.

Who is this for?

Join us for a live and interactive Q&A webinar focused entirely on child safeguarding practice. Whether you’re working in education, health, social care, early years, youth services or the voluntary sector — this session is for you.

Webinar Agenda

This webinar offers you the space to:

  • ✅ Ask your safeguarding questions, no matter how big or small
  • ✅ Learn from real case examples and live discussion
  • ✅ Explore what good safeguarding looks like in everyday practice
  • ✅ Reflect on dilemmas, thresholds, disclosures and professional curiosity
